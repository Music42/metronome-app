Highly perfomant, easy to use, minimalistic digital metronome desktop app.
Wow, that's a lot of buzz words, but what does whose mean exactly:
- First, the timer engine is implemented in a highly performant way to ensure a great experience, not causing any lags even when working the multiple apps.
- The UI is designed in a way to avoid a lot of labels to avoid cluter and to allow you to focus on whats really important:- creating.
- The tempo input supports different ways of interactions; you can use click and drag, use the scroll wheel or just enter the tempo using you keyboard with the numpads.
- Global keyboard shortcuts enable you to make changes on fly, maximizing productivity.
